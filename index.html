About Me

I am Sameer, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology, with a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). I have gained significant experience in machine learning algorithms, deep learning frameworks, and natural language processing (NLP). My journey includes working on projects like text summarization, sentiment analysis, and multi-label emotion classification using transformer models.

I am particularly focused on exploring the cutting-edge advancements in AI and generative models, with hands-on experience in fine-tuning large language models like BERT, T5, and GPT. I am also learning advanced fine-tuning techniques such as Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

In addition, I have completed an internship in the Internet of Things (IoT), where I developed IoT solutions and gained practical experience in microcontroller programming and SDLC-based project development. I am always eager to explore new technologies and apply them to solve real-world problems.
